Black Clover

Black Clover is a Japanese manga series written and illustrated by Yūki Tabata. It has been serialized in Shueisha's shōnen manga magazine Weekly Shōnen.

GRIMOIRES

A grimoire is a book that allows its user to cast specific spells that are far in advance of their own natural magical abilities. It is one of the primary sources of power for the Magic Knights and other mages on the continent of the Clover, Diamond, Heart, and Spade Kingdoms.

3 GREAT KINGDOM

Clover Kingdom World of Black Clover

The place where Asta and Yuno born.It is the home country of the Wizard King and the Magic Knight Squads. It is currently ruled by King Augustus Kira Clover XIII.

Diamond Kingdom World of Black Clover

The Diamond Kingdom lies north of the Clover Kingdom. This country of mines and wasteland is poor in resources, so it focuses its efforts on building up.

Spade Kingdom World of Black Clover

The Spade Kingdom is a country near the Clover, Heart and Diamond Kingdoms. Under the rule of the Dark Triad.
